Mathworks' MATLAB is available for use in the DesignSafe Discovery Workspace but requires a license before you can use the application. MATLAB is supported under a "bring your own license" model.

For non-UT-Austin researchers: To enable your MATLAB license on DesignSafe, please submit a request for MATLAB license activation with the following:

  • your MATLAB floating network license; and
  • your institution's MATLAB license server that allows remote access from TACC machines.

For UT-Austin researchers: To enable your MATLAB license on DesignSafe, please submit a request for MATLAB license activation with the following:

  • your college/department affiliation.

If you are unsure about the above details, please CC your MATLAB license administrator on the request.
