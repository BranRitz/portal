Disconnect Box.com

By disconnecting your Box.com account from DesignSafe-CI, you will halt any automatic syncing of files and folders between Box.com and DesignSafe-CI. This will not delete any files from either DesignSafe-CI or Box.com. What will happen is that you will be removing the authorization you granted DesignSafe-CI to manage files and folders in your Box.com account.

To reiterate, this will not delete any files. If you wish to delete the files from either your Box.com storage or your DesignSafe-CI storage you will need to do that manually.

You will still see DesignSafe-CI on your Box.com Account Security page. Be assured that by disconnecting your account here DesignSafe-CI will no longer be able to act on your Box.com account. If you wish, click "Forget App" on the Box.com Account Security page to ensure access has been revoked.
