Disconnect Dropbox.com

By disconnecting your Dropbox.com account from DesignSafe, you will no longer be able to browse files from Dropbox.com on DesignSafe. This will not delete any files from either DesignSafe or Dropbox.com. What will happen is that you will be removing the authorization you granted DesignSafe to manage files and folders in your Dropbox.com account.

To reiterate, this will not delete any files. If you wish to delete the files from either your Dropbox.com storage or your DesignSafe storage you will need to do that manually.

You will still see DesignSafe on your Dropbox.com Account Security page. Be assured that by disconnecting your account here DesignSafe will no longer be able to act on your Dropbox.com account. If you wish, click "Forget App" on the Dropbox.com Account Security page to ensure access has been revoked.
