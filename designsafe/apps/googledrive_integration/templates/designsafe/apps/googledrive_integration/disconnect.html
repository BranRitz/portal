Disconnect Google Drive

By disconnecting your Google Drive account from DesignSafe, you will no longer be able to browse files from Google Drive on DesignSafe. This will not delete any files from either DesignSafe or Google Drive. What will happen is that you will be removing the authorization you granted DesignSafe to manage files and folders in your Google Drive account.

To reiterate, this will not delete any files. If you wish to delete the files from either your Google Drive storage or your DesignSafe storage you will need to do that manually.

You will still see DesignSafe on your Google Drive Account Security page. Be assured that by disconnecting your account here DesignSafe will no longer be able to act on your Google Drive account. If you wish, click "Forget App" on the Google Drive Account Security page to ensure access has been revoked.

If you would like to disconnect your Google Drive account from DesignSafe, you can do so by clicking the button below.
